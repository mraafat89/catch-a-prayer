Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Overview

Catch a Prayer ("we", "our", "the app") is a free prayer times app for Muslims in the US and Canada. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Data We Collect

Location Data

We use your device's GPS location to find nearby mosques and calculate accurate prayer times. Your location is:

  • Used only on-device and in API requests to find nearby mosques
  • Never stored on our servers
  • Never shared with third parties
  • Only accessed when you grant location permission

Usage Data

We may collect anonymous usage statistics (e.g., which features are used, crash reports) to improve the app. This data cannot identify you personally.

Prayer Spot & Community Submissions

When you submit a prayer spot or mosque data correction, we store:

  • The information you submitted (location, prayer times, etc.)
  • An anonymous session identifier (not linked to your identity)
  • Your approximate location (to verify you're near the mosque)

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Your name, email, or phone number
  • Your religious practices or beliefs
  • Your browsing history
  • Your contacts or photos
  • Any personally identifiable information

Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services:

  • OpenStreetMap / Leaflet — Map display (their privacy policy)
  • OSRM — Route calculation (open-source, no tracking)
  • Google Maps / Apple Maps — Navigation (only when you tap "Navigate", opens external app)

Data Storage

Mosque and prayer time data is stored on our servers in the United States. No personal user data is stored on our servers.

Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect any data from children under 13. The app is suitable for all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? Contact us at privacy@catchaprayer.com
