What the App Does

Catch a Prayer helps Muslims find nearby mosques and prayer times in the US and Canada. We provide:

  • Mosque locations and contact information
  • Prayer times (scraped from mosque websites or calculated)
  • Jumuah (Friday prayer) schedules
  • Route-based trip prayer planning
  • Prayer spots submitted by the community

Accuracy Disclaimer

Prayer times shown in this app may not be 100% accurate. Times come from multiple sources:

  • Scraped data — extracted from mosque websites. Generally accurate but may be outdated.
  • Calculated data — computed mathematically based on location. These are estimates and may differ from your mosque's actual iqama times.
  • Community submissions — submitted by users. We verify where possible but cannot guarantee accuracy.

Always confirm prayer times with your local mosque, especially for Eid and Jumuah prayers.
