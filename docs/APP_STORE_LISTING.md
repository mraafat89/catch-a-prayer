## Description

Find nearby mosques, get accurate prayer times, and never miss a prayer — whether you're at home or traveling.

Catch a Prayer uses real prayer schedules scraped directly from mosque websites, not just calculated estimates. Know exactly when iqama starts at your local masjid.

KEY FEATURES:

- Find mosques near you with real-time distance and directions
- Real iqama times from mosque websites — not just calculated estimates
- Jumuah (Friday prayer) times with khutbah schedules
- Musafir (traveler) mode with Jam' Taqdeem/Ta'kheer guidance
- Trip planning: plan multi-stop routes and find mosques along the way
- Prayer spot discovery: find and share outdoor prayer-friendly locations
- Mark prayers as completed and track your daily salah
- Works across the US and Canada with 3,800+ mosques

TRAVELER MODE:
Traveling? Switch to Musafir mode for shortened prayers, combining guidance (Jam'), and mosque stops along your route. Plan your trip and Catch a Prayer finds the best mosques at each prayer time.

DATA YOU CAN TRUST:
We scrape prayer times directly from mosque websites, so you see the real iqama times your imam sets — not generic calculations. Community corrections keep data accurate.

COVERAGE:
3,800+ mosques across the United States and Canada, with more added regularly. Help us grow by submitting corrections and new mosque data.
