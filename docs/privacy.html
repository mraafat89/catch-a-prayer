Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 18, 2026

Overview

Catch a Prayer ("we", "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the app accesses and how it is used.

Data We Collect

Location Data

The app requests access to your device's location to find nearby mosques and prayer spots and to calculate travel times. Your location is sent to our server to perform these searches but is not stored permanently or associated with any personal identity.

Anonymous Session Identifier

The app generates a random, anonymous session ID stored locally on your device. This is used solely for basic app functionality. It is not linked to your identity and cannot be used to identify you.

Prayer Tracking

If you use the daily prayer tracker, this data is stored locally on your device only. It is never sent to our servers.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • We do not require or create user accounts
  • We do not collect your name, email, or any personal information
  • We do not use cookies or tracking technologies
  • We do not serve advertisements
  • We do not sell, share, or transfer any data to third parties

Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services to provide functionality:

  • OpenStreetMap / Nominatim — for map tiles and geocoding
  • Mapbox — for route directions and travel time calculations
  • Google Maps / Apple Maps — for external navigation (only when you choose to open directions)

These services may receive your location data when you use the app. Please refer to their respective privacy policies for details.

Data Security

All communication between the app and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS.

Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect any data from children under 13. The app is suitable for all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
