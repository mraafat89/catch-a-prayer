Frequently Asked Questions

Why aren't prayer times showing for my mosque?

We gather iqama times from mosque websites and community sources. If your mosque isn't listed or times are missing, please let us know and we'll work on adding it.

How accurate are the prayer times?

Each prayer time shows its source and how recently it was updated. Tap on any time to see where it comes from. We recommend confirming with your local mosque for the most accurate iqama times.

What's the difference between Muqeem and Musafir mode?

Muqeem (resident) mode shows standard individual prayer times. Musafir (traveler) mode enables prayer combining — Dhuhr with Asr, and Maghrib with Isha — as permitted for travelers in Islam.

How do I add a prayer spot?

Tap the prayer spots section and use the submit form to add a new location. Other users can verify it, and after 3 confirmations it becomes fully active.

The app can't find my location

Make sure location permissions are enabled for Catch a Prayer in your device settings (Settings > Privacy > Location Services).
